HTML5 Video Every Where

Higher performance and stable video watching experience on the web.

Some streaming sites video players suck. This add-on will replace them with browser built-in video player for higher performance and more stable experience. Currently, it supports YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, Break, Metacafe, Lego and even more sites are coming.

Developed by Moez Bouhlel, founder of Tik.
